You may already have tried:

- a new to-do app
- a morning routine
- a prioritization method
- advice found on YouTube
- a new set of shortcuts or extensions

Sometimes it helps a little.

But very often, it does not last.

Why?

Because an isolated trick does not reconfigure a full environment.

If your computer stays:

- too noisy
- too scattered
- too full of micro-decisions
- too badly organized

then even a good method eventually gets crushed by daily reality.

It is not that hacks are always useless.

It is that they are not enough when there is no underlying architecture.
