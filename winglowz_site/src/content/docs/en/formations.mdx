Want to regain control of your time, energy, tools, and attention? This is where to start.

WinGlowz Training is a complete **9-module** path designed to improve how you work in practice. It is not generic motivational content. It is a structured system for building better workflows across Windows, time management, execution, knowledge, and communication.

You can follow the curriculum in order or jump to the module that matches your current bottleneck.

---

## The 9 modules

### [I. Productivity](/en/formations/module-1-productivite/) <small>(7 pages)</small>

Before optimizing anything, you need to understand what productivity really is. This module lays the foundations: common myths, strategic thinking, focus, discipline, and the mindset behind the rest of the training.

### [II. Windows](/en/formations/module-2-windows/) <small>(7 pages)</small>

Your operating system is your cockpit. Learn how to configure Windows so it works with you: ergonomics, browser setup, tiling, automation, terminal usage, and a healthier digital environment.

### [III. Time & Energy](/en/formations/module-3-temps-energie/) <small>(3 pages)</small>

Time is limited, and energy determines what you can actually do with it. This module helps you structure your days, protect deep work, and align your workflow with your cognitive rhythms.

### [IV. Action Management](/en/formations/module-4-actions/) <small>(3 pages)</small>

Goals, projects, and tasks must work together. This module helps you clarify priorities, maintain momentum, and build a reliable execution system that survives real life.

### [V. Consumption](/en/formations/module-5-consommer/) <small>(5 pages)</small>

We all consume content. The real challenge is doing it intentionally. Learn how to filter, read, organize, and turn information into useful knowledge instead of drowning in noise.

### [VI. Knowledge](/en/formations/module-6-connaissance/) <small>(8 pages)</small>

This is the PKM module. You will learn how to capture, organize, connect, create, and retrieve information through a practical personal knowledge management system.

### [VII. Social](/en/formations/module-7-social/) <small>(6 pages)</small>

Productivity does not happen in isolation. This module covers messaging, email, meetings, social platforms, CRM habits, and collaboration without constant overload.

### [VIII. Shortcuts](/en/formations/module-8-raccourcis/) <small>(1 page)</small>

The quick-reference module: essential keyboard shortcuts and practical cheatsheets gathered in one place so you can move faster every day.

### [IX. Code on Mobile](/en/formations/module-9-mobile-coder/) <small>(4 pages)</small>

Develop from your phone with Termux, SSH, and Mosh. Lightweight setup, server connection, and workflows for coding with AI tools from anywhere.
