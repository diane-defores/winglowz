Want to regain control of your time, energy, and tools? This is where to start.

WinFlowz Training is a complete **8-module** path designed to improve how you work. It stays practical: each module is meant to give you concrete results, whether you are a student, freelancer, or employee.

Start from the beginning or jump directly into the module that matches your current bottleneck.

---

## The 8 modules

### I. Productivity

Before optimizing anything, you need to understand what productivity really is. This module lays the foundations: common mistakes, traps to avoid, and the philosophy behind the rest of the training.

### II. Windows

Your operating system is your cockpit. Learn how to configure Windows so it works with you: shortcuts, window management, environment setup, and essential tools for a smoother workflow.

### III. Time & Energy

Time is your most precious resource, and energy determines what you can do with it. Learn how to structure your days, protect deep work sessions, and align your schedule with your energy cycles.

### IV. Action Management

Goals, projects, tasks: three levels that must work together. This module helps you clarify priorities, break down larger projects, and build a reliable action system.

### V. Consumption

We all consume content: articles, videos, podcasts, newsletters. The real challenge is doing it intentionally. Learn how to filter, capture, and turn information into useful knowledge instead of drowning in it.

### VI. Knowledge

The densest module in the training. You will build your own knowledge management system: note-taking, organization, idea linking, and fast retrieval methods.

### VII. Social

Productivity does not happen in isolation. This module covers effective communication, relationship management, collaboration, and staying connected without being overwhelmed.

### VIII. Shortcuts

The quick-reference module: essential keyboard shortcuts and cheatsheets gathered in one place.

---

## Where to begin

If you are new, follow the modules in order. Each one builds on the previous one. If you already have solid foundations, start with the area creating the most friction today.
