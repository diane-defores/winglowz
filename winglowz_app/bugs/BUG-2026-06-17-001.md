# Bug Record: BUG-2026-06-17-001

## Summary

- Title: Theme Studio corner text opacity slider cannot be dragged continuously
- Current status: `open`
- Severity: `medium`
- Owner: unassigned

## Reproduction

- Preconditions:
  - Open `Keyboard Theme Studio`.
  - Expand the `Touches` section.
- Steps:
  1. Locate `Opacité du texte d’angle`.
  2. Press and drag the slider thumb horizontally.
  3. Observe whether the thumb follows the finger continuously.

## Expected Behavior

- The slider should track the drag gesture smoothly and update the draft value continuously.

## Observed Behavior

- The slider reacts to taps, but dragging does not continue smoothly.

## Diagnosis Notes

| Date UTC | Skill | Author | Hypothesis | Findings | Outcome |
|----------|-------|--------|------------|----------|---------|
| 2026-06-17 00:00:00 UTC | 106-sf-fix | Codex | A changing `ValueKey` on the `Touches` section rebuilds the subtree on every opacity change, interrupting the slider gesture. | The `Column` containing the slider was keyed by a string that included `cornerTextOpacity`, so each slider update recreated the subtree. | supported |

## Fix Attempts

| Date UTC | Skill | Author | Changes | Validation | Result | Next Step |
|----------|-------|--------|---------|------------|--------|-----------|
| 2026-06-17 00:00:00 UTC | 106-sf-fix | Codex | Removed the `ValueKey` from the `Touches` section subtree so the slider keeps its gesture state while dragging. | `flutter test test/keyboard_theme_studio_screen_test.dart` | passed | Retest in Theme Studio if needed |
