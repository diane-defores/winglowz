## Summary
- Title: Keyboard voice dictation ends with no speech detected and inserts no text
- Status: fix-attempted
- Severity: high
- Next step: /sf-test --retest BUG-2026-05-17-001

## Reproduction
- Preconditions:
  - WinGlowz Android app build installed on a physical device.
  - WinGlowz keyboard enabled and selected.
  - Microphone permission granted.
- Steps:
  1. Open any Android app with a normal text field.
  2. Show the WinGlowz keyboard.
  3. Tap the keyboard microphone action.
  4. Dictate more than one short phrase.
  5. Press Enter to finish the recording.
  6. Open WinGlowz > Voice and refresh history.
  7. Open Clipboard history.

## Expected
- Pressing Enter finishes the current dictation.
- Recognized text is inserted into the active field.
- Inserted text ends with a trailing space.
- The dictation is added to Voice history.
- The dictation is not added to Clipboard history.

## Observed
- Step 4 fails: the text is no longer entered in the active field.
- The app finishes with the message `no speech detected`.
- Step 7 fails: the voice entry is not present in Voice history.
- Step 8 passes: the voice entry is not present in Clipboard history.

## Evidence
- User report from sf-test on 2026-05-17: "4 le texte nest plus entrer dans le champs et l app termine par l info 'no speech detected'; 7 fail; 8 pass".
- No screenshot, device model, or native logs provided yet.

## Diagnosis Notes
- The regression is in the Android keyboard voice flow, likely around stop/finalization after Enter or SpeechRecognizer result handling.
- The clipboard separation behavior appears correct because the same dictation did not appear in Clipboard history.
- 2026-05-17 08:40:17 UTC | sf-fix | Codex | `RecognizerIntent.EXTRA_SPEECH_INPUT_MINIMUM_LENGTH_MILLIS` was set to 600000 ms while Enter manually calls `SpeechRecognizer.stopListening()`, so a short manually stopped dictation could finalize without usable results. | Removed the artificial minimum duration and kept the latest partial recognition as a fallback for manual stop errors/results. | supported; pending Android device retest
