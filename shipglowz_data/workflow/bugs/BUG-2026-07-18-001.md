- 2026-07-18 final local browser retest: `/fr` rendered the landing page, both expected BunnyCDN portraits returned 200, and no Unsplash/placeholder source appeared in the carousel.
- 2026-07-18 English-route retest: `/en` returned 404; `/en/roadmap` returned the expected separate English roadmap page.
- 2026-07-18 final English browser retest: `/en` rendered the landing page at desktop and mobile, both expected BunnyCDN portraits returned 200, and the carousel contained no Unsplash or placeholder source.
- 2026-07-18 production evidence: commit `919dead` deployed READY on Vercel; `winflowz.com` redirects to `www.winflowz.com`; `/fr` and `/en` return 200; both public BunnyCDN portrait URLs return 200; Diane confirmed the real images and testimonials visually.
- 2026-07-18 source evidence: `Hero.astro` independently declared five `/images/headshots/` images despite the widget's already-authenticated Florin and Alex portraits.
