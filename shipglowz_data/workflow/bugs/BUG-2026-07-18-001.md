## Reproduction

- Preconditions: homepage testimonial section is rendered.
- Steps:
  1. Load the homepage testimonial carousel.
  2. Inspect the portrait sources and browser CSP result.
  3. Compare the two BunnyCDN origins with public GET requests.

## Expected Behavior

- The homepage presents the testimonial carousel with only traced, authentic portraits.
- Public BunnyCDN portraits load under the deployed CSP.

## Observed Behavior

- The local French homepage `/fr` now renders the landing page, including the testimonial carousel. At 1280×720 Florin Muresan's BunnyCDN avatar is visible; at 390×844 Alex's BunnyCDN avatar is visible.
- The English homepage `/en` now renders the landing page. At 1280×720 Florin Muresan's complete BunnyCDN avatar and English testimonial text are visible; at 390×844 Alex's complete BunnyCDN avatar and English testimonial text are visible. No French text was observed in the widget.
- The local technical retest does not replace Diane's required human validation and must not be treated as a resolved, fixed, verified, or closed claim.

## Evidence

- `src/pages/[...lang]/index.astro` renders `LogoMarquee`, which mounts the testimonial carousel.
- `src/components/react/landing/TestimonialCarousel.tsx` contains the stock and placeholder sources.
- Public URL checks: `https://winflowz.b-cdn.net/Florin_muresan.jpg` (200 image/jpeg); `https://winflowz.b-cdn.net/alex-dynapictures.png` (200 image/png).
- Public URL checks: matching `https://winglowz.b-cdn.net/` paths (403).
- 2026-07-18 local browser retest: `/fr` displayed `Feuille de Route` without the carousel; the root landing page rendered the two intended BunnyCDN avatar URLs successfully.
- 2026-07-18 final local browser retest: `/fr` rendered the landing page, both expected BunnyCDN portraits returned 200, and no Unsplash/placeholder source appeared in the carousel.
- 2026-07-18 English-route retest: `/en` returned 404; `/en/roadmap` returned the expected separate English roadmap page.
- 2026-07-18 final English browser retest: `/en` rendered the landing page at desktop and mobile, both expected BunnyCDN portraits returned 200, and the carousel contained no Unsplash or placeholder source.
